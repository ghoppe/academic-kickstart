Notes for Irene Dorothy Anaweiler
PENNER, Irene Dorothy (nee Anweiler) - Was born April 4, 1920 in the Dundurn, SK area. She passed away on November 18, 2016 at Pineview Lodge in Nipawin, SK. Irene married William (Bill) Penner on March 29, 1974. Together they adopted two daughters, Donna and Tammy. Bill and Irene took care of Karen Howard and Todd Antonenko for a number of years and treated them as if they were their own children. Irene was a busy homemaker. She babysat for friends and took care of her children. She loved cooking, baking, gardening, tending to her flowers, crocheting, knitting and embroidery. She also enjoyed jig saw puzzles, playing cards and going to yard sales on Saturdays. Irene belonged to the Salvation Army, took part in the Woman's Auxiliary and helped in the thrift store. After Bill passed away, Irene sold her house and moved to Cedar Manor and lived there until October of 2011 when she moved into Pineview Lodge because of her health. A funeral service was held on November 23, 2016 at 2:00pm at Heritage Funeral Home, Nipawin, SK. Condolences to the family may be expressed by email at info@heritagefuneral.ca or online at www.heritagefuneral.ca Arrangements for the late Irene Penner have been entrusted to HERITAGE FUNERAL HOME in Nipawin, SK.
