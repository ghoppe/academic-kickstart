Notes for Stanley Finice Mattoon
This young Flight Sergeant, member of the RCAF, was llisted as missing when his plane was shot down on Dec. 17, 1942. Seven years later, in 1949, the area in which the plane had gone down, north of Urk, on the coast bordering the Zuider Zee in North East Polder, Holland, was reclaimed from the sea. During the excavation, the plane was discovered. The bodies of the three crewmen were identified and burial took place in the Amersfoort Rusthof Cemetery, 26 miles south of Amsterdam.1
