Notes for Wilhelmina Hagel
18 August 1938, OBITUARY, MRS. WILHELMINA HOPPE, The death of Mrs. Wilhelmina Hoppe, at the great age of 96, occurred at the home of her daughter, Mrs. F. Brenner of the Welland district, on Friday, August 12.
The funeral was held Sunday, August 14, from Zion Lutheran Church to Woodlawn Cemetery, Nipawin, where interment was mad. Rev. L. W. Koehler officiated. He took his text from Deut. 15:4,5.
Three sons and one daughter survive to mourn for her: Fred and John of Nipawin, and August of Langenburg, and Mrs. Brenner. There are also 28 grandchildren and 27 great grandchildren.
Deceased came to Canada from Poland 30 years ago, and before comming to Nipawin four years ago, resided at Watrous.
Pallbearers were Charlie, August and William Hoppe, Henry and Gust Detbrenner and Henry Rother.
Mitchell’s Funeral Home had charge of the funeral arrangements.1695
