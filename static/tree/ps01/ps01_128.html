Notes for Bernice Denomme
Bernice Marie (Denomme) Moore was born May 1st, 1934 to Lawrence & Verda (Rau) Denomme in St. Joseph, Ontario. Bernice was the 2nd youngest of 8 children and was also a loving mother of 7 children, and enjoyed her many grandchildren and great grandchildren. In between raising her family, farming, working various jobs and volunteering, Bernice was an active member of the Foresters, a competitive curler, and an enthusiastic euchre player. Well known among her family & friends for her community support, generosity and her freezer full of goodies. Always having a strong faith, Bernice was most recently devoted to the Trinity Christian Reformed Church. Bernice passed away peacefully in her home in Goderich, Ontario on Feb. 27th 2015 with her family by her side. Bernice was in her 81st year.
