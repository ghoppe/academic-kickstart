Notes for Eric Austin Moore
MOORE, Eric - Eric was a loving father, respected teacher, enthusiastic farmer with many interests and hobbies who had a positive influence and outlook throughout his whole life. Passed away peacefully at Alexandra Marine and General Hospital in Goderich on Sunday, January 15, 2012. Eric Austin Moore of Benmiller in his 86th year. Survived by Bernice Moore. Dear father and father-in-law of Sherry and Herb Marshall, John and Helene Moore, Morgan Moore all of Goderich, Lisa and Paul Markle of Toronto, Margo Moore and Scot Little, Richard Moore and Adam and Julie Moore all of Goderich. Grandpa to Jordan, Tyler, Adam, Brianna, Jesse, Mark, Wesley, Katelyn, Blake, Hannah, Blake, Isabella, Bennett and Eric. Brother and brother-in-law of Ronald Moore and his partner Dagmar Romano of Hillsburgh, Alf Tait of Nanaimo, B.C. and Jean Moore of Goderich. Predeceased by his brother Tom Moore, sister Betty Tait and granddaughter Maxine. Friends were received at FALCONER FUNERAL HOME LTD. - Bluewater Chapel , 201 Suncoast Drive, East, Goderich, (519-524-1221) on Tuesday, January 17th from 2-4 and 7-9 p.m. where the funeral service was held on Wednesday January 18th, 2012 at 1 p.m. with Tom Dunbar officiating. Cremation. As expressions of sympathy, the family would appreciated memorial donations to the Diabetes Association or the Heart and Stroke Foundation. Condolences may be sent to the family through ffhbwchapel@hotmail.com
