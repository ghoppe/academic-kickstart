Notes for Olive Marie Masse
Peacefully, surrounded by her family, at Bluewater Rest Home, Zurich, Olivia Marie (Masse) (Denomme) Bedard passed away on Wednesday August 5, 2015 in her 100th year. Beloved wife of the late Dennis Denomme (1955) and the late George Bedard (1984). Dear mother of Martha and the late Willy Overholt of Zurich and Cecelia and Don Denomme of Goderich. Loving grandmother of Vicky and Randy Geoffrey, Steve and Deb Overholt, Patti and Steve Geoffrey, Debbie Regier and friend Ron Crete, Paul and Colleen Overholt, Cathi and Marc Clausius, Peter and Stephanie Overholt, Jacqui Denomme, Jeff Denomme and Julie Denomme and great grandmother of 29 and great great-grandmother of 22. Dear sister-in-law of Grace Masse, Orlee Masse, Lucille Masse and Ethel Masse. Sadly missed by Mark Regier, Ann Harvey, her many nieces and nephews and the Bedard and Denomme families. Predeceased by her parents Phillip and Myrtle (Meidinger) Masse, one sister Jean Guilmette and brothers Leonard (Margaret) Masse, Elzer (Beatrice) Masse, Adelore Masse, Clare Masse and Bill Masse
