Sammit, Anna Passed away at the Milton District Hospital on Wednesday, August 15th 2007. Anna Sammit of Milton, beloved wife of the late George Sammit. Loving mother of Joyce Royce. Anna will be missed by her grandchildren Sheila and her husband Kevin Blacklock, Susan Royce, Sheryl and her husband John McNiven, Sheldon Royce and her great grandchildren Laura and Joel Blacklock and Everett, Alec and William McNiven. Dear sister of Minnie Frisky. Family and friends are invited to visit at the McKERSIE-KOCHER FUNERAL HOME 114 Main St. Milton 905-878-4452 from 7-9 pm on Friday. The funeral service will be held in the funeral home chapel on Saturday, August 18th 2007 at 11:00 am. Interment to take place at Glen Oaks Memorial Gardens. Memorial donations to the Milton District Hospital Foundation or the Canadian Cancer Society would be appreciated. Letters of condolence may be left for the family at www.mckersie-kocher.ca
