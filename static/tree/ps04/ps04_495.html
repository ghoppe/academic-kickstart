Notes for Shirley Denomme
DENOMME, Shirley 1937 - 2010 Passed away on Sunday, October 3, 2010 after a brief illness at St. Mary’s General Hospital age 73 years. Loving wife of John Denomme of Waterloo. Dear mother of Roger and his wife Cindy of Brantford, Shawn and his wife Roma of Kitchener. Will be sadly missed by grandchildren Nathan, Dylan and MacKenzie. Also survived by a brother Valere and his wife Grace of Arva, sisters Marie Oldham of London, Marjorie Gamble and her husband Wayne of Etobicoke and Donna Rau of Norwood. Predeceased by her parents Margaret and Edward, a son Kevin in 1975, brother Jerome, sisters Delaine (Joe) Cronin, Doris (Michael) Meyer, brothers-in-law Clare and Joe and a sister-in-law Marie.
