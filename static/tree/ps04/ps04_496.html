Obituary for Donna Rau
At Campbellford Memorial Hospital, Trent Hills, Ont, on Tuesday, Aug 13, 2012, Donna Jean (Denomme) Rau, passed away peacefully in her 72nd year. Beloved wife of the late Joseph Charles Rau (1995). Dear mother of Deborah (Terrance) Companion of Nova Scotia and Joanne (Dean) Hawkes of Norwood. Loving grandmother of Tammy and Edward Soper, Amanda Companion, Kristin Rau, Sarah Rau, Brady, Samantha, Melissa and Kyle Hawkes. Great-grandmother of Koleton, Reigan and Rylan Soper and Noah Rau. Dear sister and sister-in-law Margorie and Wayne Gamble, Valare and Grace Denomme, John Denomme. Sadly missed by her many nieces and nephews and the Edward Rau family. Predeceased by her parents Edward and Martina (Regier) Denomme, one son Daniel, brother Jerome (Marie) Denomme, sisters Marie (Clark) Oldham, Shirley Denomme, Doris Meyer and Delaine Cronin. Visitation will be held in the J. M. McBeath Funeral Home, 49 Goshen St. N., Zurich, Ont on Wednesday from 6-9 p.m. The Funeral Mass will be celebrated on Thursday, August 16, 2012 at 11 a.m. in St. Boniface Roman Catholic Church, Zurich, Ont. Fr. Graham Keep celebrant. Interment St. Boniface Roman Catholic Cemetery. Parish prayers will be said at 8:30 p.m. Donations may be made to the Canadian Cancer Society Condolences forwarded to www.jmmcbeathfuneralhome.com
A tree will be planted as a living memorial to Donna Rau
