Notes for Joseph Laurence Denomme
A resident of Pain Court, Joseph Dénommé, suddenly passed away surrounded by his family on Sunday, December 16, 2012 at the Chatham-Kent Health Alliance, Chatham Campus. Born 81 years ago in St. Joseph, Ontario son of the late Telesphore & Antoinette Dénommé. Beloved husband to Blanche Dénommé (nee Barnier). Cherished and loving father to Barry Dénommé & Tammy Barker, Betty Dénommé & Wilfred Rivait. Papa will be forever missed by his grandchildren Amy, Samantha, and Ryasan Dénommé, Caleb & Coleton Dénommé. Survived by his sisters Monica Barron (James), Thérèse Waters (Joe), and Claire Robertson (Bernard). Predeceased by siblings Charles Dénommé, Margaret Koekuyt, Cyril Dénommé, Leo Dénommé, Robert Dénommé, and Cecile Dénommé. Forever missed by many nieces and nephews. Joe was a long time member of Immaculee Conception Catholic Church and the Immaculée Conception Knights of Columbus Council 9693.
