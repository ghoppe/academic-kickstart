Dear mother of the late Joseph Denomme, Stephen Denomme and Leeta York of Abbotsford, B.C., Deborah and Jim Gavan of Camlachie and Frederick Denomme of Calgary, AB. Sadly missed by her close family and friends Sue and Gerhard Frank, Julie and Doug Debus, Jean and the late Dante Bauer, Ross and Avonne White, Lorne and Donna Schultz and Sandra and her many nieces and nephews.
