Notes for Arthur Dennis Hoppe
The passing of Mr. A. Dennis Hoppe of Biggar, SK. occurred peacefully with his family at his side in the Biggar Hospital on July 27, 2015, at the age of 66 years. Dennis is survived by his wife, Theresa of Biggar, SK., two daughters: Laura (Neal) Maier of Regina, SK. and Allison (Neil) Isley of Delisle, SK., and their children, Colby and Barret; four sisters and three brothers, numerous nieces and nephews and extended family. Funeral Services will be held on Wednesday, August 5, 2015, at 11:00 a.m. at the Redeemer Lutheran Church with Rev. Mark Kliener officiating. Memorial Donations in Dennis' memory may be directed to the Redeemer Lutheran Church Memorial Fund or the Canadian Cancer Society. Grondin Funeral Services- Biggar entrusted with arrangements (948-2669).
